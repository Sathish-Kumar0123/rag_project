# Events Organised by cse vrsec ,Summary 2022–23.

During the academic year 2022–23, the Department of Computer Science & Engineering organized a total of 30 events including seminars, workshops, FDPs, guest lectures, student competitions, and certification programs.

- Seminars/Webinars: 7
- Workshops: 13
- FDPs: 3
- Guest Lectures: 3
- Others: 4

The events focused on emerging technologies such as IoT, Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing, Blockchain, AR/VR, and industry-oriented skills. Participation included students, faculty, and non-teaching staff. Most events were sponsored by SAGTE, with some supported by AICTE.
